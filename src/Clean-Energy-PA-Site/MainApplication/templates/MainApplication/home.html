Did you know that PA state law allows you to switch energy suppliers from PECO?
And - you can actually switch to 100% renewable

Clean Energy PA is an open-source application made by graduate students at Penn State.

The service is free, and aims to help make it easier to sign up and track for suppliers - finding an initial green rate at below PECO's price, keeping track of your contract lengths and alerting you when cheaper ones are available, or when your current contract is about to expire.

It's 100% free, the code is open-source, and all data is kept private.
